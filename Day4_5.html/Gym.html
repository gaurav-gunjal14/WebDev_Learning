Split Training: 7-Day Gym Workout Plan

This 7-day workout plan is designed for individuals who want to focus on different muscle groups each day, allowing for adequate recovery while maximizing strength and hypertrophy.

Weekly Split Overview

  • Day 1: Chest
  • Day 2: Back
  • Day 3: Shoulders
  • Day 4: Biceps
  • Day 5: Triceps
  • Day 6: Legs
  • Day 7: Rest

Monday: Chest Workout

  • Bench Press – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Chest Dips – 3 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Cable Flyes – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Push-Ups – 2 sets to failure

Tuesday: Back Workout

  • Pull-Ups – 4 sets × 6–8 reps
  • Barbell Rows – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Lat Pulldowns – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Deadlifts – 3 sets × 6–8 reps

Wednesday: Shoulder Workout

  • Overhead Barbell Press – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Front Raises – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Arnold Press – 3 sets × 8–10 reps

Thursday: Biceps Workout

  • Barbell Curls – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Hammer Curls – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Concentration Curls – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Preacher Curls – 3 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Cable Curls – 3 sets × 12–15 reps

Friday: Triceps Workout

  • Close-Grip Bench Press - 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Skull Crushers – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extensions – 3 sets × 10–12 reps

Saturday: Leg Workout

  • Squats – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Lunges – 3 sets × 10–12 reps per leg
  • Leg Curls – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Calf Raises – 4 sets × 12–15 reps

Sunday: Rest Day

Take this day to rest and recover. Focus on hydration, nutrition, and light stretching or yoga to help your muscles recover.
